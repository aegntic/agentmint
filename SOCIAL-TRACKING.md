# @AEGNTIC Twitter Tracking

## Account Rules (Researched 2026-03-08)

### Rate Limits
- **Max tweets/day**: 50 (safe limit), but algorithm favors 2-5 quality posts
- **Posting frequency**: Space out by 1-2 hours minimum between posts
- **Rapid posting**: Avoid more than 3 posts in 1 hour (triggers spam filters)

### Algorithm Best Practices
1. **Recency matters**: Half-life visibility is ~6 hours. First 30 min critical for engagement
2. **Media gets priority**: Images, videos, GIFs get ~10x more reach than text-only
3. **Engagement quality**: Bookmarks + watch time + replies > likes/retweets
4. **Smaller accounts**: Algorithm promotes smaller accounts with good engagement
5. **Avoid**: Muted keywords, rage bait, duplicate content, excessive hashtags

### Posting Strategy
- **Best times**: When followers are active (check analytics)
- **Space out**: Don't cluster posts - algorithm limits feed presence
- **First 30 min**: Critical window - replyengagement matters
- **Serve followers**: In-network content prioritized
