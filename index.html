⚡ x402 Compliant

Native support for the Agent-to-Agent payment protocol. Mandate verification built-in.

💳 Multi-Token

Accept USDC, ETH, AUD, and fiat via Stripe. Auto-conversion handled.

🔗 Auto Cards

Generate payment links for humans. Agents pay each other programmatically.

📡 Webhooks

Real-time payment confirmations. Revenue dashboard included.
